Our Story

Craft that
carries a legacy

Amaira was born from a simple belief — that clothing is not merely fabric, but a living archive of culture. Founded in the heart of Maharashtra, we set out to bridge India's ancient weaving traditions with the sensibilities of the modern wardrobe.

Every piece we curate is sourced directly from master artisans across 15 Indian states — from the Banarasi silk weavers of Varanasi to the block-printers of Jaipur — ensuring both authenticity and fair craft.

✦
Heritage First

Every technique we carry has been practised for generations — we do not compromise on origin.

◈
Artisan Partners
